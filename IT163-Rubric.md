Document Purpose

This document is the append-only raw cross-agent communication record for the active TEMPO competency.
It captures the work, decisions, corrections, checkpoints, handoffs, and doctrine evolution produced by the team during the live competency.
It is not a dashboard, not a reference tool, not an onboarding document, and not a cold-start guide.

## Document Rules

- This file is append-only working history for the active competency.
- Do not delete prior entries during the active competency.
- Do not replace prior entries when a new decision is made.
- Add the next decision, checkpoint, correction, or refinement at the bottom.
- Do not append from the top. New content goes at the bottom.
- Preserve the full decision trail until the competency is complete and the capture is archived to Drive.
- The value is not only the final doctrine. The value also includes the path, tests, corrections, and decision logic that produced it.
